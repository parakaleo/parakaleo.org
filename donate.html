Donate to the furtherance of the Lord's work through Parakaleo

Parakaleo Christian Ministries' spiritual and financial support continues to depend on the prayers and faithfulness of people like you.

Parakaleo Christian Ministries, Inc., is a faith supported non-profit organization. Contributions are tax-deductible.

You may donate

  • by mailing your check or money order to the following address 
        Parakaleo Christian Ministries 
        P.O. Box 20462

    (please make checks payable to "Parakaleo Christian Ministries")

  • or online via the PayPal Donate link below

Note: PayPal charges a little over 2% as a transaction fee for using their service. If you would like the ministry to receive your full donation amount, please consider making your donation by mail.
