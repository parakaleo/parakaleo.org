A Gift of Thanksgiving for 50 Years of Ministry

Your contribution will enable Parakaleo Christian Ministries to continue serving students at Stanford and beyond.

For 50 years, Parakaleo has faithfully ministered through the prayers and generous support of people like you. As we celebrate this milestone, we invite you to consider a special gift of thanksgiving that will sustain our ongoing work.

Parakaleo Christian Ministries, Inc., is a faith supported non-profit organization. All contributions are tax-deductible.
